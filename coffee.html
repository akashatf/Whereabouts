Coffee Places

Sue's Gallery Cafe

Super cute cafe in the quiet Saratoga downtown area! The location is beautiful - surrounded by nature and green everywhere. There is a very peaceful vibe, even though the cafe is usually quite busy, especially during peak hours.

14665 Big Basin Way, Saratoga, CA 95070

Crema

It's also a nice place to study with free wifi (password located on the bottom of your receipt). Staff is friendly and service is usually quick. Drinks and food are tasty and affordable.

950 The Alameda, San Jose, CA 95126

Sightglass Coffee

Sightglass Coffee is one of many third wave coffee shops in San Francisco and this 7th street shop is Sightglass' flagship location (established in 2009). Unlike most other coffee shops and three other Sightglass Coffee physical locations, this branch has a hanging bike rack at the front entrance as well as the affogato bar upstairs.

270 7th St, San Francisco, CA 94103

Timeless Coffee

One of my friends shows me this gem-- absolutely one of my favorite places for coffee and baked treats. I've come here three times since my friend took me the first time and every time I am more and more impressed. I have yet to have something I wasn't impressed with!

4252 Piedmont Ave, Oakland, CA 94611

Bitter + Sweet

This place is pretty cozy and would be a great place to grab a cup of coffee/latte/hot cocoa in the cold weather.

20560 Town Center Ln, Cupertino, CA 95014

Artis Coffee

Fast forward in time and you find Artis Coffee. This place has the best beans in town, and they pair them with some really good and healthy offerings, like gluten-free stuff. This space is also super cool and away from the madness. If you are in the area, check them out. They also sell beans.

1717 Fourth St B, Berkeley, CA 94710

Suju's Coffee

What a great place to study and work! Free WiFi, plenty of seating, good food and drinks, and best of all, the music volume was perfect -- not too loud where you can't focus.

4949 Stevenson Blvd, Fremont, CA 94538

Blue Bottle Coffee

I am a Blue Bottle fanatic and this location is probably my favorite. It's in an incredible spot with so much seating and this indoor / outdoor setting, with two actual coffee bars you can get service from.

456 University Ave, Palo Alto, CA 94301

Red Giant Coffee

BEST COFFEE IN THIS AREA. parking is a bit tricky around here since it is downtown RWC but every time I'm in sequoia station for lunch or groceries, I try to drop by and get a latte here.
