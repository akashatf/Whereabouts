Hiking Spots

Mission Peak

Climb all the way up to the wooden post at the tip top of Mission Peak! This hike is around 6 miles long.

43600 Mission Blvd, Ohlone College Fremont, CA 94539

The Dish

The Dish is a 4 mile loop that has amazing views of the Bay. It gets hot, so try to go in the morning.

Stanford Ave & Junipero Serra Blvd, Palo Alto, CA 94305

Los Gatos Creek Trail

The trail is filled with trees and flowers and ends in Vasona Park. There are several different paths to take.

1250 Dell Ave, Campbell, CA 95008

Land's End Lookout

One of the prettiest ocean views in the city. Land's End gets a bit chilly, so bring a jacket!

680 Point Lobos Ave, San Francisco, CA 94121

Sierra Vista

The sunrises are amazing at Sierra Vista! You can also bike the trail.

5341 Sierra Rd, San Jose, CA 95132

Russian Ridge

Russian Ridge has amazing views of the Bay's skyline. If you come near sunrise or sunset, you can see the clouds cascading over the mountains!

Redwood City, CA 94062

Handley Rock

Handley Rock is filled with greenery. Everyone loves to climb the rocks, too!

Handley Trail, Emerald Hills, CA 94062

Tilden Regional Park

There are trees, flowers, cacti, waterfalls and so much more at Tilden Regional Park. Makes for a great hike!

2501 Grizzly Peak Boulevard, Orinda, CA 94563

Sibley Volcanic Regional Park

Sibley has amazing views of the Bay and tons of nature.

6800 Skyline Blvd, Oakland, CA 94611

Picchetti Ranch Open Space Reserve

Picchetti has ponds, trees, steams, and shady trails. An amazing hiking area!

13100 Montebello Rd, Cupertino, CA 95015
